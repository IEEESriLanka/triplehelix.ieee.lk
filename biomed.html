Biomedical Engineering Track

The Biomedical Engineering is the latest specialized track added to the roundtable. Although this sector was not discussed as a separate track last year, it was a significant part of the discussion in Electronics Engineering sector. This year, we are excited to see what the Biomedical Engineering as a dedicated track will explore with its growing importance and impact in the field of engineering and technology.
