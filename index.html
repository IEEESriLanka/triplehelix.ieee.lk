Bridging Innovation: The Triple Helix Model in Action

This Roundtable unites visionary leaders from Sri Lanka’s key sectors, creating a collaborative platform that bridges industry, academia, and government. Guided by the Triple Helix Model of Innovation, we aim to drive a unified innovation strategy that accelerates national development and sustainable progress.

Industry

Powering economic growth by turning research and innovation into real-world solutions and market value.

Academia

Advancing research, nurturing talent, and driving discovery to power next-generation technologies.

Government

Shaping policy, enabling investment, and aligning national priorities to build a strong and competitive innovation ecosystem.
