Date:  24th November 2025

Venue:  Waters Edge

Bridging Innovation: The Triple Helix Model of Innovation in Action

This Roundtable unites visionary leaders from Sri Lanka’s key sectors, creating a collaborative platform that bridges industry, academia, and government. Guided by the Triple Helix Model of Innovation, we aim to drive a unified innovation strategy that accelerates national development and sustainable progress.

Industry

Driving economic growth, identifying market needs, and applying cutting-edge research to create real-world solutions and commercial value.

Academia

Fostering foundational research, developing skilled talent, and serving as the engine of discovery for next-generation technologies.

Government

Creating supportive policy, enabling strategic investment, and aligning national objectives to build a robust and competitive innovation ecosystem.

Past Success: Roundtable 2024

Key Roundtable Tracks 2025

Delegates will join one of six specialized tracks for in-depth, sector-specific discussions.

Electronics and Smart Manufacturing

Innovations in hardware, smart production, IoT, and building resilient supply chains.

IT & Software

Leveraging AI, data science, cybersecurity, and software engineering to power the digital economy.

Power & Energy

Focusing on renewable energy, smart grids, sustainability, and energy policy for self-sufficiency.

Telecommunications

Exploring 5G adoption, digital infrastructure, connectivity, and the future of communication.

Biomedical Engineering

Integrating engineering and medicine to advance healthcare technology and medical devices.

Geo Science

Utilizing satellite imagery, GIS, and remote sensing for infrastructure and resource management.
